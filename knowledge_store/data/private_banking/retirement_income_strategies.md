Content:
Retirement income strategies focus on converting accumulated
assets into sustainable income during retirement.

Financial advisors often evaluate withdrawal rates, portfolio
allocation, and expected lifespan when designing retirement
income plans.

Common income sources include dividends, bond interest,
annuities, and systematic withdrawals from investment
portfolios.

Tax planning also plays a key role in retirement income
strategies, as withdrawals from different accounts may
be taxed differently.

Proper retirement planning ensures that investors maintain
financial stability throughout retirement.
