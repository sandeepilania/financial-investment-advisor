Title: Liquidity Planning for High Net Worth Investors

Key Points:
- Liquidity planning ensures access to cash when needed.
- Illiquid investments may limit financial flexibility.
- Advisors balance liquid and illiquid assets.
- Proper liquidity planning reduces financial stress.

Content:
Liquidity planning involves ensuring that investors have
sufficient access to cash or liquid assets to meet financial
obligations and opportunities.

High-net-worth investors often allocate capital to
illiquid investments such as private equity or real estate.

While these investments may provide higher returns,
they may also limit immediate access to funds.

Financial advisors therefore maintain a balance between
liquid and illiquid assets to ensure flexibility for
unexpected expenses, investment opportunities, or
lifestyle needs.
