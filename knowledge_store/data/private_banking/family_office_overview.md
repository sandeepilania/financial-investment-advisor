Key Points:

- Family offices may oversee tax planning and philanthropy.
- They coordinate long-term wealth management strategies.

Content:
A family office is a private wealth management structure
that serves the financial needs of affluent families.

Family offices provide services such as investment
management, estate planning, tax strategy, and
philanthropic planning.

Single-family offices manage the wealth of one family,
while multi-family offices serve several clients.

These organizations aim to preserve and grow wealth
across generations while providing comprehensive
financial oversight.
