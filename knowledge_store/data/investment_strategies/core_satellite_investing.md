Title: Core Satellite Investing Strategy
Category: investment_strategies
Tags: core_satellite, portfolio_strategy, diversification
Risk Profile: moderate
Target Audience: intermediate
Investor Horizon: long
Last Updated: 2026-03

Key Points:
- Core holdings form the stable foundation of a portfolio.
- Satellite investments seek additional growth opportunities.
- Core assets are typically index funds or diversified ETFs.
- Satellite assets may include sector funds or active strategies.

Content:
The core satellite investing strategy combines a stable
foundation of diversified investments with smaller
positions in higher-growth opportunities.

The core portion of the portfolio typically consists
of broad market index funds or ETFs designed to
capture long-term market returns.

Satellite investments represent smaller allocations
to specialized sectors, emerging markets, or active
investment strategies.

This approach allows investors to maintain portfolio
stability while pursuing additional returns through
targeted investments.
